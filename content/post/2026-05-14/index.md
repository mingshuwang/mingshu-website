---
title: Dr Mingshu Wang selected for national DAFNI Fellowship
date: 2026-05-14
summary: "Dr Mingshu Wang has been named a DAFNI Fellow to develop GeoAI tools for UK infrastructure analysis. His work will support more transparent, reusable, and evidence-based infrastructure decision-making."

---
Dr Mingshu Wang, Reader in Geospatial Data Science at the University of Glasgow, has been selected as one of ten new [DAFNI Fellows](https://www.dafni.ac.uk/who-we-are/dafni-fellowship-programme/dafni-2026-fellows/), following a nationally competitive process that attracted 116 applicants.

The fellowship will support his project, “GeoAI-enabled workflows for decision-ready infrastructure evidence”, which will develop analysis-ready datasets, reproducible models, and transparent workflows for scenario-based analysis of UK infrastructure systems. The project will contribute to the [DAFNI platform](https://www.dafni.ac.uk/) by supporting reusable and evidence-based infrastructure analytics for decision-making across academia, government, and industry.

Each DAFNI Fellow has been awarded £10,000 to support staff time, travel, knowledge exchange, conferences, and dissemination activities. The fellowship will run from 1 April 2026 to 30 June 2027, during which Dr Wang will engage with the wider DAFNI community through workshops, conferences, and collaborative activities focused on infrastructure resilience and interdisciplinary research.
